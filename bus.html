🔍 Introduction

Douyin combines entertainment and e-commerce, using AI to drive personalized content and boost business engagement. Learn how technology transforms online shopping in China.

✅ Advantages & ❌ Disadvantages

💡 Personalized AI Content

Boosts exposure, targets specific audiences, and improves ROI.

⚠️ Limited Discovery

Can narrow product diversity and create excessive consumption cycles.

🎥 Seamless Livestream Shopping

Users watch and shop without leaving the stream, enhancing convenience.

🔐 Privacy & Overconsumption Risks

Algorithmic decisions may affect user satisfaction and data safety.
